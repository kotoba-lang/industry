# Engineering Industries

The registry includes engineering/manufacturing enablement entries so that
future open businesses can use existing kotoba-lang technical work. Two
worked examples follow (2610, 3030); the full generated coverage table below
lists every implemented engineering/heavy-industry/mining ISIC entry across
mining and extraction (divisions 05-09) and basic metals through industrial
repair/installation (divisions 24-33).

## ISIC 2610

Semiconductor and electronics manufacturing enablement:

- EDA for design and verification
- CAE for simulation evidence
- BPMN/DMN for process and decision governance
- audit ledger for traceability

## ISIC 3030

Aircraft and aerospace manufacturing enablement:

- CAE and CFD for simulation and certification evidence
- optimization for planning and scheduling
- BPMN/DMN for controlled processes
- audit ledger for certification proof
